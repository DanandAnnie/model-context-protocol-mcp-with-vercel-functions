Our Programs

Music for Every Age

From first sounds to full performances — find the perfect program for your child.

🎴

Sound Beginnings

Ages 0–4

Early childhood music for babies through preschoolers (with caregiver). Morning sessions with singing, movement, and instrument exploration. Flexible scheduling with back-to-back class times.

🎈

Let's Play Music

Ages 4–6

The core Let's Play Music program. A comprehensive musical foundation covering ear training, chord theory, classical music appreciation, piano, note reading, and solfege.

🎹

Presto

Ages 7–12

An accelerated piano class for beginners. Proper piano technique, ear training, chord theory, music history, solfege, and composition come together to produce rapid results.

🎸

Guitar

Ages 7–16

Group guitar classes for kids and teens. Learn chords, strumming, and songs in a supportive group environment.

🎺

Ukulele

Ages 5–12

A fun and accessible introduction to string instruments. Perfect for younger musicians ready to strum their first chords.

Every Child Deserves Music

Research shows that music education improves cognitive development, builds social skills, and boosts confidence. Give your child a gift that lasts a lifetime.
