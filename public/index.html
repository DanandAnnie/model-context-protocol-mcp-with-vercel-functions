Our Programs

Sound Beginnings

Ages 0–4

Early childhood music with singing, movement, and instrument exploration. Flexible morning sessions.

Red Balloons

Ages 4–6

Bridging early childhood into structured music education for pre-K and kindergarteners.

Presto

Ages 7–12

Comprehensive music education covering piano, theory, and ensemble skills in group settings.

Guitar

Ages 7–16

Group guitar classes learning chords, strumming, and songs in a supportive environment.

Ukulele

Ages 5–12

A fun and accessible introduction to string instruments for younger musicians.
