Privacy Policy

Staging Inventory Manager — Last updated: March 2026

What We Collect

When you use Staging Inventory Manager, we collect only what's needed for the app to function:

  • Account info: Email address and password (for authentication)
  • Inventory data: Items, properties, storage units, photos, and staging history you enter
  • Financial data: Payment records and expenses you enter for tax tracking

How Data Is Stored

Your data is stored in a Supabase-hosted PostgreSQL database with row-level security. Data is also cached locally on your device for offline access using IndexedDB.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your inventory and financial data is only accessible by you and team members you explicitly invite.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database hosting, authentication, and real-time sync
  • Vercel — app hosting
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — optional AI features for item identification (photos are sent to the API only when you use AI features)

Your Rights

You can export or delete all your data at any time from within the app. To request complete account deletion, contact us at the email below.

Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Authentication uses Supabase Auth with bcrypt-hashed passwords. Row-level security policies ensure users can only access their own team's data.
