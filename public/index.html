Daily Video Script Generator

MCP Server for local content creators — powered by Vercel Functions

Recommended Workflow

  1. Call search_local_news for your city
  2. Call search_local_events for events and openings
  3. Call search_new_businesses for up-and-coming spots
  4. Call fetch_real_estate_insights for market data
  5. Pass everything into generate_daily_video_script
  6. Film, edit, post!

Available Tools

search_local_news

Pull local news, happenings, and stories for any city via Google News RSS. No API key required.

search_local_events

Find grand openings, ribbon cuttings, chamber of commerce events, festivals, and community gatherings.

search_new_businesses

Discover new and up-and-coming businesses, restaurants, and commercial developments in your area.

fetch_real_estate_insights

Get real estate market trends, home prices, new developments, and investment opportunity news.

generate_daily_video_script

Combine all gathered content into a structured video script — short-form (TikTok/Reels) and long-form (YouTube) — with hooks, segments, transitions, and CTAs.

get_weather

Get current weather for any location — useful for video intros and local context.

Connect via MCP: https://your-deployment.vercel.app/mcp
